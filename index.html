Rythu Bazars were established in the year 1999 with an objective of direct sale of vegetables by the farmers without intermediaries andto provide quality vegetables to the consumers at reasonable prices.
101 Rythu Bazars are functioning in the State with temporary/ semi-permanent structures. No service charges or market fee is levied for sale of vegetables in Rythu Bazars. Joint Collector is the Co-ordinating officer of Rythu Bazars . At present, 100 Rythu Bazars are functioning in the state.
